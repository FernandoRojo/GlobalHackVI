Contact

If you are in need of emergency shelter or food, be sure to text 805-399-2192 your current address to get turn-by-turn directions to the nearest facility that can help.

Alternatively, call 314-802-5444 for a intake specialist with the Emergency Shelter Hotline for St. Louis County. The Toll Free number is 1-866-802-7155, and the line for those with Hearing or Speech Impairments is 314-802-5348.
